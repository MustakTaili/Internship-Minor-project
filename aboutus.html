Company profile


Garnet Motors (India) Pvt ltd is founded by Mr K.S Cheema in year 2016. After successful operations of the Mercedes-Benz dealership in Nagpur,, Mr Cheema took over two more Mercedes-Benz locations in the renowned cities of Gujarat. i.e Ahmedabad & Surat in year 2020. His avid interest in luxury automotive business brought great success to Garnet Motors.


Mr K.S Cheema hailing from a farmer’s family of gangheri village in Kurukshetra district of Haryana, braved his way to the current position of a successful top management executive against all odds with sheer hard work and determination. He is a dynamic professional & successful entrepreneur with formal education in management and law. He’s been a successful Independent lawyer, in house Legal Counsel, Director Business & Projects Development, deal maker and Board member of a number of MNCs and expert in setting up and managing international joint ventures.


Our Group Slogan is "Delivering Smiles" and team Garnet Motors believes in making each moment memorable for every Mercedes-Benz owner.We are a team of skilled professionals backed by vast experience and passion about automobile.With over 2000 smiles delivered by Garnet Motors in a short span of time, we have become the most preferred dealer for Mercedes-Benz passenger cars.
