The new EQS.
Complimenting the future.

With the new EQS, we are looking at the future, responsibly. Sharp, clean lines designed to perfection, the EQS spells style all the way. This all-electric vehicle is next gen with personified luxury, while made for everyday use. The new EQS is an electric car like no other with technology tailored to suit every purpose, every need and every whim. It attends to you and adapts to your habits as it drives you into a new tomorrow.

Easy charging for easy driving.

With a unique high voltage battery and an innovative charging system, the new EQS can be recharged in no time at all at 200kW power sources. The EQS has an EPA estimated driving range of 350 miles with a charging ecosystem offering nearly 60,000 public charge points nationwide with the assurance of green energy. You can even start the day charged at home with the Mercedes-Benz Wallbox which allows you to charge your vehicle easily at home within a few hours.
